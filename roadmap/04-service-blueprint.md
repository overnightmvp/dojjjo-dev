# 04. Service Blueprint // THE LOOP FACTORY

To deliver a $2k/mo service profitably, this backend must be an assembly line, not an art studio.

## The 3-Phase Fulfillment
### Phase 1: Digital Foundation (Days 1-7)
- **The Audit:** Identify "Broken Entities" (Schema, Robots.txt, machine-readability).
- **SME Interview:** 30-minute extraction to feed the AI content engine.
- **The Handshake:** Deployment of `llms.txt`.

### Phase 2: Signal Injection (Days 8-21)
- **Concept Mapping:** 4 Technical Blog Posts + 2 Service Pages.
- **PR Placement:** Higher-DA background links (DA 60-75) to act as "Trust Anchors."

### Phase 3: Validation & Outbound (Days 22-30)
- **Cold Outreach:** SDRs hit the phone using the "AI Gap Audit" results.
- **Authority Report:** Documentation of new citations in LLM responses.

## Team Fulfillment Roles
| Role | Responsibility | Allocation |
|---|---|---|
| **Account Architect** | Initial SME interview & Oversight | 2hrs/mo |
| **Prompt Engineer** | Content & Schema Automation | 4hrs/mo |
| **PR Coordinator** | Mid-tier news site placements | 3hrs/mo |
| **Closer (SDR)** | 50 calls using the AI Gap Script | 10hrs/mo |
