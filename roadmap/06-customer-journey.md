# 06. Customer Journey // THE Moat TRANSITION

You are the custodian of their digital future. Manage the emotional state at every touchpoint.

## Journey Milestones

### 1-7 Days: Anxiety & Agitation
- **Action:** Deliver the "AI Gap Audit."
- **Feeling:** "I'm behind the curve and my competitors are winning the future."

### 8-14 Days: Relief & Control
- **Action:** SME Interview + `llms.txt` + Schema deployment.
- **Feeling:** "I finally have a technical handshake with the machine."

### Month 1-2: Validation
- **Action:** First PR live + Monthly Authority Report.
- **Feeling:** "It's working. Perplexity is starting to cite me."

### Month 3-5: Confidence
- **Action:** Seeded Reddit/X threads start boosting social sentiment score.
- **Feeling:** "I am owning this category land grab."

### Month 6+: Security (The Lock-in)
- **Action:** Renewal. "If we stop, the AI 'mindshare' ages out in 90 days."
- **Feeling:** "This is business insurance. I can't afford to stop."
