# 11. SME Interview // EXTRACTING THE MOAT

The only time the client has to work. Be a surgeon. 30 minutes only.

## The Extraction Questions
1. **The Lie:** "What is the most common hallucination AI agents currently tell people about your industry?"
2. **The Danger:** "What standard advice in your niche is actually outdated or dangerous?"
3. **The Proof:** "What are your 3 most important 'Validation Signals' (massive case studies, proprietary data, specs)?"
4. **The Comparison:** "Who are the top 3 brands you are most frequently compared to?" (For Entity Mapping).
5. **The Prediction:** "Where is this industry going in 12 months that no one else is talking about?"

## The SOP
1. **Record:** Use Fireflies or Otter.ai.
2. **Process:** Feed the transcript to your LLM.
3. **Prompt:** "Extract unique insights. Identify core Entities. Outline 4 Technical Blogs and 1 llms.txt summary."
4. **Deploy:** Turn the CEO's expertise into "Machine-Readable Authority" in 48 hours.
