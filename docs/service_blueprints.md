## Blueprint 02: The Trust Authority Loop
*The "Moat" Engine*

### Phase 1: The Digital Foundation (Week 1)
- **Front:** Onboarding call + SME Interview (30 mins).
- **Back:** Auto-transcribe interview into Content Roadmap.
- **Technical:** Deploy `llms.txt`, JSON-LD, and semantic sitemaps.
- **Metric:** Indexing status in crawler logs.

### Phase 2: Signal Injection (Weeks 2-3)
- **Front:** Monthly content approval (single touchpoint).
- **Back:** "Prompt Factory" generates 4 Technical Protocol assets from SME data.
- **Technical:** Trigger High-DA PR Placement via publishing partners.
- **Metric:** Backlink live status.

### Phase 3: Validation & Scale (Week 4)
- **Front:** Delivery of the "Authority Report."
- **Back:** Manual query verification across 3 major LLMs.
- **Upsell:** Transition to $5.5k Tier (Link Injection/Ads).
- **Metric:** Share of Model (SoM) growth.
