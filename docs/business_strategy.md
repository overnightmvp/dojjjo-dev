# Business Strategy: Caption - The Trust Authority Loop

## 1. The ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) Research-Backed

Based on 2026 market trends and Reddit/Web sentiment analysis, we are targeting three distinct "Vulnerable Authorities":

### Segment A: The "Vulnerable Category Leader" (Series B/C SaaS)
- **Who:** Scaling SaaS companies with $5M+ ARR and a defined category (e.g., "Hyper-automated CRM").
- **Their Hell:** ChatGPT and Perplexity are recommending their venture-backed competitors or "AI wrappers" because those competitors have higher "Entity Velocity." Every AI query is a lost sales opportunity.
- **The Heaven:** "Category Authority." We force the AI to cite them as the definitive standard for their niche.

### Segment B: The "Ghost Authority" (High-Ticket Consulting)
- **Who:** Boutique consulting firms or individual SMEs (Subject Matter Experts) with high-value IP.
- **Their Hell:** Their blogs and whitepapers are being scraped and summarized by LLMs, but they receive zero attribution or referral traffic. The AI is "stealing" their brain.
- **The Heaven:** "IP Protection." We use Signal Injection (PR) to link their brand entity to the specific concepts the AI is currently summarizing.

### Segment C: The "Invisible Specialist" (Industrial / Technical Mfg)
- **Who:** Manufacturers or technical service providers with deep catalogs and specs.
- **Their Hell:** Procurement officers are using Gemini/ChatGPT to find parts/specs. Because their site is "read-only" (PDFs/legacy HTML), the AI ignores them or provides inaccurate specs.
- **The Heaven:** "Technical Authority." Their data becomes the "Ground Truth" that the AI uses to answer technical procurement queries.

## 2. Brand Strategy: "The Last Advantage"

### Visual Identity
- **Aesthetic:** "Industrial Authority." 
- **Palette:** Deep Charcoal, Neon Cobalt (for technical highlights), and High-Contrast White. 
- **Vibe:** Strategic, clinical, brutally honest. Zero fluff. No "smiling business people" stock photos. Use data visualizations and 3D technical schematics.

### Messaging Hooks
- "If AI isn't citing you, your sales team is invisible."
- "The AI's mind is being made up right now. Are you a source or just noise?"
- "Google ranking is for 2024. Entity Moats are for 2026."

## 3. Service Blueprint (The Factory)

### Service 1: Hyper-Targeted Outbound (Legacy Boost)
- **Core Value:** Immediate feedback loop and meeting booking.
- **Blueprint:**
    1. **Data Enrichment:** Use Clay to find companies in the ICP with high PR but low AI citation share.
    2. **The Script:** The "AI Gap" script (reporting the visibility hole).
    3. **The Goal:** 50 calls/mo to validate the "Trust Authority" messaging and book demos.

### Service 2: The Trust Authority Loop (The New Core)
- **Phase 1 (Days 1-7):** Technical Handshake (llms.txt, JSON-LD, Robots.txt optimization).
- **Phase 2 (Days 8-21):** Signal Injection (4 Technical Blog Posts + 1 High-DA PR Placement).
- **Phase 3 (Days 22-30):** The Loop Validation (Share of Model report + AI Citation screenshots).
