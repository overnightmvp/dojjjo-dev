# Launch & Scaling: From First Client to $10k+ Retainers

## 1. The 7-Day Launch Sprint
[ ] **Day 1:** Build list of 50 "Invisible" companies (High PR, 0 AI Citations).  
[ ] **Day 2:** Update LinkedIn/Twitter with the "Authority Protocol" messaging.  
[ ] **Day 3:** Start "AI Gap" calls/DMs. Goal: 5+ Loom Audit requests.  
[ ] **Day 4:** Record and send Looms. Focus on the "Indexing Vulnerability."  
[ ] **Day 5:** Close calls for the $2,000 "Baseline" package.  
[ ] **Day 6:** Fulfillment kickoff (SME interview & Technical Handshake).  
[ ] **Day 7:** Invoice payment and Project Start.

## 2. Scaling & Upsell Scripts

### Upsell A: The "Dominance Injection" ($3.5k bump)
"The infrastructure is ready. Now we need velocity. Your competitor has a head start. We can force the AI's hand in 30 days with our 700+ Link Injection. Do you want to wait or own the category now?"

### Upsell B: Full Sales Cycle ($10k+ /mo)
"We're booking the meetings, but your internal team isn't trained on the 'Authority Close.' We'll handle the lead from AI query to signed contract. We provide the SDRs and AEs."

## 3. Performance Tracking (Success Metrics)
- **SoM (Share of Model):** Appearance % in 10 unique Perplexity/ChatGPT queries.
- **Citation Fidelity:** Accuracy of the AI's explanation of your USP.
- **Entity Sentiment:** Strength of association between Brand Name and Target Category.
