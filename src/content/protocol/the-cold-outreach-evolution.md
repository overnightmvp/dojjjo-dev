**Definition:** A sales strategy that uses factual technical vulnerabilities (like being invisible to AI) as the primary hook. It positions the SDR as a consultant reporting a problem rather than a vendor seeking a meeting.

## Why is the 'Discovery Call' dead?

**Definition:** Modern buyers already know their "problems." They are looking for "Verified Fixes." Asking discovery questions is seen as a waste of time by high-status founders compared to providing an immediate audit.

**Definition:** Positioning the AI Visibility Gap as a risk to shareholder value or company reputation, making it the CEO's "Fiduciary Responsibility" to fix the technical handshake.
