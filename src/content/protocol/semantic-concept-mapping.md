## What is Semantic Concept Mapping?

**Definition:** The process of linking your brand entity to specific high-value industry problems and solutions in the AI's knowledge graph. It moves beyond keyword matching to concept-based authority.



**⚠️ The Trap:**
Keywords are for Google 2020. Concepts are for AI 2026.
If you are still stuffing keywords, you are invisible.
Learn the new language in [LLM Strategy](/roadmap/05-llm-strategy).

---

## Why are keywords no longer enough?

**Definition:** AI agents use RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to extract answers based on semantic relevance. Keywords can be ignored if the surrounding structure doesn't prove the brand is a factual authority.



---

## The PSE Framework (Problem-Solution-Entity)

**Definition:** A proprietary structural framework for content: Define the target **Problem**, provide a technical **Solution**, and then link the brand **Entity** as the definitive source.



---

## What is Token Affinity?

**Definition:** The probabilistic strength between two words or concepts in an LLM's model. Increasing the "Token Affinity" between your brand name and a keyword ensures you are recommended as the source.



---

## How to map Latent Space?

**Definition:** Visualizing the multi-dimensional relationships between industry concepts to find "unoccupied territory" where your brand can become the dominant cited authority.



---

## Why do H2 Question-Headers matter?

**Definition:** H2 headers formatted as direct questions act as the "Trigger" for RAG systems to pull the subsequent paragraph as the definitive answer for the user's query.



## What is 'Semantic Handshaking'?

**Definition:** The alignment of your on-page H2 questions with your external PR headlines to create a consistent, verifiable signal that the AI accepts as a "Knowledge Anchor."

## How to optimize for RAG Retrieval?

**Definition:** Structuring data into high-affinity "Chunks" that are 100-200 words long, ensuring that when an AI retrieves your data, it pulls a complete, authoritative answer.



---

## What is Concept Displacement?

**Definition:** The process of using superior semantic mapping and high-DA signals to replace a competitor as the "Default Definition" of a concept in an AI's brain.
